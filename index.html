📖 ETCC Silent Auction Manager — User Manual

This application manages the silent auction for the East Tennessee Corvette Club. Follow the six steps in order for each auction event.

Step 1 — Item Load

Scans etccwebsite.auction@gmail.com for donation emails. Extracts item fields and loads them into the items database. Duplicate emails are skipped. Each item receives a unique item number based on its category.

Step 2 — Create Bidding Sheets

Generates a printable bidding sheet for each item showing item number, description, starting bid, bid increment, and a table of bid rows.

Step 3 — Bidder Registration

Register attendees as bidders. Import member numbers from a Club Express file. Send emails to individual or all bidders.

Step 4 — Auction Close

After the auction closes, enter the winning bidder number for each item using bulk entry mode.

Step 5 — Winner Announcement

Generates a list of all winning bidders and items that can be announced to attendees.

Step 6 — Payment & Pickup

Payment report listing each winning bidder, items won, and payment details. Sortable by bidder number or last name.

Settings

Enter your Google OAuth Client ID to enable Gmail scanning. Configure the field mapping table to match your email format.
